Ferret Man

A Web/Game Dev

I'm Ferret Man, a web and game developer with a knack for crafting digital experiences that bring joy and excitement. My journey in development started at a young age, tinkering with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to bring my ideas to life on the web.

As a kid, I dipped my toes into game development using Scratch, and by the age of 8, I was hooked. From there, I ventured into Python and Pygame, exploring the endless possibilities of game creation. Today, I continue to learn and grow, driven by my passion for coding and my love for building cool stuff. When I'm not glued to my screen, you'll find me exploring new tech, diving into gaming worlds, or simply enjoying the great outdoors.
